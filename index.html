About JAPS

Welcome to Jeejal Amaan Public School, Bhittaiabad, a beacon of educational excellence that began its journey on December 7, 2021. Led by a dedicated team under the visionary leadership of Mr. Manzoor Ahmed Soomro, our school is committed to providing quality education to students in the rural community of Bhittaiabad. From its inception, Jeejal Amaan Public School has strived to create a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically and personally. With modern facilities such as a state-of-the-art science lab, a fully equipped computer lab, and an engaging activity room, we aim to provide our students with the resources they need to succeed in the digital age.
